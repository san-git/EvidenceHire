# EvidenceHire Roadmap

## Step 1. Foundations

- Define product scope and unique value prop.
- Create repo structure, landing page, and match stub.
- Set up environments and hosting defaults.

## Step 2. Auth and projects

- Supabase auth with email magic link.
- Projects table with roles and candidate batches.
- Basic roles and permissions.

## Step 3. Ingestion

- Resume and JD upload (PDF, DOCX).
- Text extraction and normalization.
- Skill and keyword extraction.

## Step 4. Matching engine

- Embedding generation and caching.
- Similarity scoring across roles and candidates.
- Evidence extraction and gap analysis.

## Step 5. Review workspace

- Ranked results with evidence trails.
- Filters by score, skills, and gaps.
- Export to CSV and shareable links.

## Step 6. Beta readiness

- Usage tracking and error monitoring.
- Basic billing guardrails.
- Feedback loop and in-app prompts.

## Step 7. Commercialization

- Pricing tiers and billing.
- Team features, SSO, and analytics.
- Marketplace integrations and ATS connectors.
